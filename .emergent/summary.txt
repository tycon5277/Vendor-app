**Key Technical Concepts**
-   **Zone-Based Architecture:** Hyperlocal delivery zones (circles/polygons) for controlled operations. Genies and vendors are locked to specific zones.
-   **Server-Sent Events (SSE):** For real-time, one-way push of delivery requests to Genies, replacing inefficient polling at scale.
-   **Redis:** Used extensively for:
    -   **Pub/Sub:** For SSE message brokering on a per-zone basis.
    -   **Caching:** Caching order statuses to reduce DB load from Wisher app polling.
    -   **GEO Data Structures:** Storing and querying Genie locations for fast, zone-aware proximity searches.
    -   **Queuing:** Managing the queue of orders for the background assignment engine.
-   **Background Tasks (asyncio):** The assignment engine runs as a non-blocking background task to handle the entire sequential assignment loop automatically.
-   **Score-Based Assignment:** Prioritizing Genies based on distance, rating, acceptance rate, and idle time.
